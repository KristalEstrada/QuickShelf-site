About QuickShelf

INVENTORY MADE EFFORTLESS

QuickShelf is a lightweight inventory platform designed for small businesses that need accurate, up‑to‑date counts without the enterprise bloat. We focus on clarity, speed, and reliability. So you can spend more time serving customers and less time fixing spreadsheets.

Our Story

QuickShelf started in a small market that needed a clear view of stock across shelves and storage. We built lightweight tools that worked on any device and scaled features as we learned from real‑world use.

Roadmap

  • Barcode scanning & SKU search
  • Location mapping & multi‑site support
  • Low‑stock alerts & reorder suggestions
  • Simple analytics & CSV/Sheets export

Accessibility & Inclusion

We aim for WCAG‑friendly contrast, keyboard navigation, and clear language so everyone on the team can manage inventory with confidence.
